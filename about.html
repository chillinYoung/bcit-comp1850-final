Welcome to BCIT Computing Community!

This community is to share information and study materials among BCIT Computing students. You can join us to access the numerous contents and to ask any help to peers!
The contents might contain what school provided, and in this case, verified students only can access to them. You can verify your identity by providing a copy of student ID in your account setting.

Prospective Menu

This menu is for prospective student who are interested in applying to BCIT Computing Programs.
The tips are here, which were provided by current or formal student. Thus, information are unofficial, and might not accurate. All information is for the references. Please contact to the BCIT admission office for the official answers.

Campus-life Menu

This menu is only for the current and graduate students. You can help each other and find tips about classes, personal projects, and making career path. Also, selling used textbooks are possible.

Careers Menu

The job postings are categorized by cities.
Some of data are automatically crawled from job sites and realated companies' sites (in this case, the sourse will be listed in the posting), and you can post one by your own as well. Please be aware of that this community does not have any benefits to post about jobs and also doesn't have any responsibilities about any issues caused by your personal contact with job providers.

If you need more details about programs, go to BCIT official web site.
